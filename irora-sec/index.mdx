## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ingest" icon="download">
    Irora SEC monitors EDGAR in real time. New filings are indexed within 300ms of publication. Form 4 and STOCK Act disclosures are parsed and classified automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process" icon="microchip">
    AI models extract sections, parse XBRL data, identify entities, and generate summaries. The change detection engine compares each filing to its predecessor. Average processing time is 2.3 seconds per filing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Score" icon="chart-line">
    Each filing receives a risk score (0-100) based on anomaly detection across financials, disclosures, and historical patterns. Section-level changes are classified by a 17-rule severity engine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Serve" icon="bolt">
    Query, search, extract, and stream via purpose-built APIs. Set up watchlists for the tickers you care about and receive real-time alerts via webhooks or in-app notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>
