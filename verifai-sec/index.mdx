## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ingest">
    VerifAI SEC monitors EDGAR in real time. New filings are indexed within 300ms of publication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process">
    AI models extract sections, parse XBRL data, identify entities, and generate summaries. Average processing time is 2.3 seconds per filing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Score">
    Each filing receives a risk score (0-100) based on anomaly detection across financials, disclosures, and historical patterns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Serve">
    Query, search, extract, and stream via 34 purpose-built APIs. Results are cached and served from a global CDN.
  </Step>
</Steps>
