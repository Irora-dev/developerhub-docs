## Limits by tier

Rate limits vary per product and subscription tier. Below are the standard tiers for VerifAI SEC.

| Endpoint       | Free    | Personal | Business | Enterprise |
| -------------- | ------- | -------- | -------- | ---------- |
| Query API      | 2/sec   | 20/sec   | 40/sec   | Custom     |
| Full-text search | 1/sec | 10/sec   | 20/sec   | Custom     |
| Section extractor | 1/sec | 10/sec  | 20/sec   | Custom     |
| Filing download | 1/sec  | 15K/5min | 15K/5min | Custom     |

### Bandwidth limits

In addition to request rate limits, each tier includes a monthly bandwidth allowance:

| Tier       | Bandwidth      | Overage      |
| ---------- | -------------- | ------------ |
| Free       | N/A            | N/A          |
| Personal   | 50 GB/month    | $0.30/GB     |
| Business   | 100 GB/month   | $0.30/GB     |
| Enterprise | Custom         | Custom       |

## Burst allowance

All tiers include a brief burst allowance of 2x the sustained rate for up to 5 seconds. This accommodates startup traffic spikes without triggering a `429`.
